# **App Name**: AdCraft Studio

## Core Features:

- Homepage Design: A clean and visually appealing homepage to showcase the company's services and brand.
- Works Slideshow: A dynamic slideshow of the company's best outdoor ad works, with high-quality images and descriptions.
- Contact Form: Contact form with fields for name, email, and message, enabling potential clients to reach out easily.
- AI Add Copy Suggester: AI-powered tool that can generate sample ad copy ideas based on an uploaded picture of the location where the add would go. The AI will use location-appropriate, concise, and creative wording suggestions for the client to pick from, or expand.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Rich red (#C8232B), echoing the logo, conveying energy and professionalism.
- Background color: Soft off-white (#F2F0EC), to contrast with the red elements and add a sense of sophistication. 
- Accent color: Deep red (#A21922), an analogous tone, is used on buttons, to create good contrast without distracting from other parts of the website
- Headline font: 'Poppins' (sans-serif) for a contemporary, fashionable, avant-garde look.
- Body font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for readability and a touch of warmth in longer texts.
- Use modern, minimalist icons in red or dark gray (#333333) to represent services, contact information, and other key features.
- Emphasize large, high-quality images of outdoor ad works with clear, concise descriptions.
